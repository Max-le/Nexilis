"Smarter Wi-Fi credentials" ?

Wi-Fi credentials are the SSID ( the "name" of your network ) and the password needed to connect to Internet. But most of the time, passwords are long and tricky to type in.

Qonnect proposes you to use the new technologies to make this easier.

QR codes

QR codes are everywhere : they're easy to use and compatible with most smartphones.

Time saver

It's easier and more convienent for your customers to scan a QR code instead of typing in a long password

Static or dynamic

Static codes store your data and don't change over time. Dynamic codes can be modified and edited remotely if needed, for example if you change network provider. Dynamic codes also to get some useful data about your customers.

NFC-enabled

The QR codes also includes a NFC tag (for "near field communication"). A mobile phone with a NFC chip can connect to your network just by simple contact.
